ABOUT ME



Welcome to the captivating world of me, a multifaceted artist based in the heart of Tbilisi. With an innate passion for both photography and graphic design, I weave visual narratives that breathe life into moments and ideas.


My lens is a window to the soul of Tbilisi, capturing the city's vibrant spirit, rich history, and diverse culture. Through photography, I aim to freeze time, preserving the unique charm of every scene, from the cobblestone streets of the Old Town to the modern skyline that defines the city's evolving identity.


As a graphic designer, I bring creativity and innovation to every project. Whether crafting compelling branding for local businesses or designing visually striking digital content, my goal is to elevate and amplify the essence of each concept. I believe in the power of design to tell stories, evoke emotions, and leave a lasting impression.


Driven by a relentless pursuit of excellence, I combine technical skill with a keen eye for aesthetics. Each project is a canvas, and I approach it with a dedication to perfection and an unwavering commitment to bringing ideas to life. I understand that every client and every story is unique, and I tailor my work to reflect that individuality.

Beyond the pixels and frames, I am deeply rooted in the Tbilisi creative community. Collaborations with fellow artists, entrepreneurs, and visionaries fuel my inspiration and drive me to push the boundaries of my craft. I believe in the transformative power of art to connect people, bridge cultures, and ignite change.
Explore my portfolio and join me on a visual journey through Tbilisi and beyond. Whether you seek captivating photography that tells a story or innovative graphic design that leaves a lasting impression, I am here to turn your vision into a captivating reality. Let's create something extraordinary together.
